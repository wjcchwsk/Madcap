Overview

Hello and welocome to our beautful city

Located in southern-western Poland in amazing Sudety Mountains, Szczawno-Zdroj, the famous spa-city is waiting for You. If You likie to good food, calm nature and take a little break - that's the right place.

Place to visit

  • Park with mineral water degustation room, theather, collections of trees and plants.

  • Lookout tower on the top of Gedymin's Hill.

  • Another lookout tower on the top of Chelmiec peak.

  • Horse ranch with caffe, exclusive restaurant, alpaka's farm and other fun attractions.

  • Swimming pool with sauna, sport complex for flat tracks sports, skatepark,hardcore mountain bike track.

  • And much more :)
