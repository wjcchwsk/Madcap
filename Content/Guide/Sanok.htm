Sanok

Sanok, nestled in the picturesque valley of the San River, is a city with a rich history and a unique character.

Its location at the crossroads of various cultures has shaped its heritage over the centuries and makes it a special place on the map of Poland today.

 

History and culture

Sanok boasts a long history dating back to the Middle Ages. The city was an important trade and cultural center, which is reflected in its architecture and urban layout.

 

  • Old Town: Historic tenement houses, the town hall, and churches create a charming atmosphere in the old town.

  • Sanok Castle: An impressive building that has witnessed many historical events.

  • Historical Museum: Housed in the restored castle, it presents a rich collection related to the history of the region.
